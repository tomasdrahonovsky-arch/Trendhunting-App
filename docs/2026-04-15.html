The lesson: consumers will find their way to authentic experiences, bypassing gatekeepers if necessary.

For non-alcoholic beverage builders, the opportunity lies in the space between health surveillance and hedonistic abandon—drinks that facilitate intentional pleasure, support social connection, and respect the consumer's growing desire for agency without demanding constant self-improvement. The brands that win will be those that understand moderation not as deprivation, but as a form of self-knowledge.
